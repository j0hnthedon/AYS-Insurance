State and Federal Resources and Programs

Extra Help

A program specifically designed to help financially qualified beneficiaries pay some or all Medicare Part D premiums, Part D late enrollment penalties, deductibles, copayments, and coinsurance. The assistance amount provided varies depending on a personâ€™s situation and is meant to assist those with limited income and resources.

Medicare Savings Programs

    Medicare Savings Programs specifically designed to help pay some or all of Part A and Part B premiums. Depending on the program the Medicare beneficiary qualifies for, it may also cover deductibles, copayments, and coinsurance. There are four types of Medicare Savings Programs.

  • Qualified Medicare Beneficiary Program (QMB)
  • Specified Low-Income Medicare Beneficiary Program (SLMB)
  • Qualifying Individual Program (QI)
  • Qualified Disabled and Working Individuals Program (QDWI)

    • The beneficiary will automatically qualify for Extra Help if approved for QMB, SLMB or QI.

Program of All-Inclusive Care for the Elderly (PACE) Program

    A Medicare and Medicaid program that provides medical care and social services allowing the beneficiary to live safely in their community with assistance. To qualify, you must:

  • Live in the plane service area
  • Be a minimum of 55 years old
  • Need nursing home level of care, as certified by the state
